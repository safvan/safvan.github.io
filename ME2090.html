🗒Syllabus

  • Definitions - mobility and degrees of freedom, kinematic pairs, kinematic chains, mechanisms and machines
  • Inversions of kinematic chains; Kennedy’s theorem; instantaneous center of rotation
  • Position, velocity and acceleration analysis - graphical and analytical
  • Synthesis of mechanisms; cams - translating knife edge, roller and flat face followers, offset
  • Gears - terminology, fundamental law of gearing; involute profile, interference, undercutting, minimum number of teeth, contact ratio; bevel gears, helical gears, worm gears; gear trains - simple, compound, and epicyclic.

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Mechancial and Aerospace Engineering in the Indian Institue of Technology, Hyderabad (IIT-H).

My research interests are concerned with developing engineering applications by understanding and controlling the nonlinear solid mechanics arising from large displacements. I particularly focus on design problems rooted in mechanics of slender structures, compliant mechanisms, structural optimization, and interdisciplinary areas.

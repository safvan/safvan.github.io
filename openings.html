Postdoctoral candidates

  • Please contact through email with a full CV and letter of intent/project idea. Individuals will be provided support towards applying for national postdoctoral fellowships (NPDF through SERB, etc). Candidate will be supported for their career development and mentored to achieve their career goals.

    • PhD candidates

  • Students interested in joining as PhD scholars can apply through IIT Hyderabad call for PhD in Mechanical and Aerospace Department. Please check IIT Hyderabad and MAE, IIT Hyderabad websites for updates regarding the admissions. Students are also encouraged to apply for project positions by sending brief CV and a statement of purpose.

    • JRF

  • [Closed] 27-01-2022 Applications are invited for the post of Junior Research Fellow (JRF) for a SERB sponsored project for a duration of 1 year.
  • [Closed] 11-05-2021 Applications are invited for the post of Junior Research Fellow (JRF) for a sponsored project.

    • Summer interns

  • Interested students can apply through IIT Hyderabad funded summer internships or through the Indian Academy of Science (IAS). Please email project idea and/or a letter of interest along with a brief one-page CV.

    • UG/Masters students at IIT Hyderabad

  • Students should send an email if interested in working in the lab.

    • UG/Masters/Dissertation students at Institutions other than IIT Hyderabad
